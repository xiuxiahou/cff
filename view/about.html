ABOUT

关于我们

定位

Positioning

中国互联网金融科技基金是专注投资全球范围内互联网金融科技公司的 私募股权投资基金，主要投资在大中华区、欧洲、北美等国家和地区、 处于发展扩张或成熟阶段的互联网金融科技公司
China FinTech Fund (“CFF”) targets equity or equity-related investments in internet financial technology companies at growth expansion phase and mature stage mainly in China, Europe, U.S., and other regions.

缘起

Origin

中国互联网金融科技基金由中民国际资本有限公司（“中民国际”）、 广发投资（开曼）有限公司（“广发投资”）和瓴睿资本（“瓴睿资本 ”）共同发起成立，致力于通过互联网金融科技，改变金融服务的业态 模式，提升金融辐射的深度和广度，引领互联网金融的科技革命
China FinTech fund was founded by CM International Capital Limited ("CM International"), GF Investments (Cayman) Company Limited ("GF Investment") and L.R. Capital Group ("LRC"), devoted to the advancement of internet finance through broadening and deepening the scope of internet financial technology, with an aim to enhance efficiency and quality of financial services.

愿景

Mission

利用互联网金融科技的创新，颠覆和改造传统的金融服务模式，发现和 挖掘互联网创新企业并帮助其快速成长
The mission of CFF is to transform and revolutionize innovative financial services companies that possess cutting edge technologies and help them grow quickly
