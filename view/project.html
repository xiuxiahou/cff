About

Dianrong.com

点融是中国互联网金融科技基金在中国投资的第一家互联网金融科技项目

点融网是一家中国领先的创新互联网金融平台，由全球最大的网络借贷平台LendingClub联合国创始人苏海德创办

点融网的股东包括中国互联网金融科技基金、渣打银行、老虎基金、马云家族基金、新鸿基金融、领睿资本、北极光等

Dianrong.com is the first company CFF invested in in China

Dianrong.com is a leading internet finance platform, founded in 2012 by the co-founder and former Head of Technology of Lending Club, Mr. Soul Htite. It aims to combine internet technology and finance innovation, constructing a highly efficient, transparent, user friendly online finance platform

Dianrong.com’s shareholders includes: China FinTech fund,Standard Charted, Tiger Global Management, Jack Ma’s family fund, Sung Hung Kai Financial, L.R. Capital, Northern Light Venture Capital .etc
